Rungano, the MoW bus, captures the power and essence of humanity’s desire to document familial and communal traditions, culture, history, and science. Parents, siblings, family, and neighbors are the first “storytellers” who teach children the richness of their environment, the art, and the science of this enduring human technology, which establishes a foundation for children emerging as lifelong learners.
