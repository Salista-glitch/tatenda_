tatenda

Tatenda Blessing Muchiriri (he/him) is an AMS Emerging Leaders Fellow, visionary Montessori innovator, and transformative coach and speaker.

Tatenda revolutionized access to Montessori education with his groundbreaking project, Rungano Montessori on Wheels, a liberatory approach that brings quality Montessori learning directly to children and families. Born in Zimbabwe and enriched by years in China, Tatenda’s diverse experiences fueled his passion for education and leadership. Now a respected board member with the Colorado Montessori Association and a key figure on the MECR Teacher Education Program board, he leverages his expertise to empower educators and inspire change at educational forums nationwide. Through dynamic coaching and engaging speaking engagements, Tatenda ignites innovation, nurtures leadership, and challenges the status quo—helping leaders and communities unlock their full potential.
